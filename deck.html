Engineering Depth

Precision Alignment & Acoustic Matching

Temporal Drift Correction

Transcription and diarization pipelines produce timestamps with systematic offset. The orchestrator tests -150ms to +150ms in 10ms steps, maximizes word-segment overlap, and applies correction if drift exceeds 20ms.

Smart Segment Selection

For voiceprint extraction, selects the optimal ~5s non-overlapping segment per speaker. Falls back to concatenation of adjacent segments within 0.5s gap. Ensures clean embeddings without speaker interference.

Acoustic-Semantic Fusion

Pre-computed voiceprint matches are injected as evidence into the agent's context. The agent fuses these with transcript-level cues: self-introductions, name mentions, role references, and attendee list proximity.

Orphan Recovery

Words that fall between diarization segments are recovered within a ±300ms window and assigned to the nearest speaker. Overlap regions are detected and flagged for the agent.

Resilience

Multi-Level Fallback Cascade

Every phase is designed to degrade gracefully. If the GPU service is unavailable, Mistral API picks up. If the agent fails to resolve speakers, threshold-based acoustic matching takes over. The pipeline never hard-fails.
